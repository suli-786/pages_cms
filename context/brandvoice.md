# **Ummah Tech Brand Voice – Quick Reference Guide**

---

## **Essence of Our Voice**

- **Faith-rooted** → Qur’an & Sunnah guided, purposeful, authentic.
- **Professional** → credible in tech, future-focused, solution-driven.
- **Visionary & Collaborative** → inspiring, unifying, community-first.

**Always:** warm, respectful, inspiring, clear.

---

## **Tone & Style by Context**

### **1. Visionary / Professional**

*For website, media releases, partner decks, formal invites*

- Polished, credible, future-facing.

- Forward-looking words: *ecosystem, catalyst, purpose-driven.*

- Tie everything back to mission & impact.

**Example:** “Ummah Tech is more than a conference—it is the beginning of a global ecosystem of Muslim technologists committed to building purposeful solutions for humanity.”

### **2. Community**

*For WhatsApp, alumni updates, volunteers*

- Warm, inclusive, approachable.

- Emojis welcome ✅

- Short, clear, action-driven.

**Example:** “Salaam Ummah Tech family 🌙 We’re kicking off weekly panels to spark ideas and grow together—come add your voice!”

### **3. Inspirational / Heritage**

*For social media, campaigns, storytelling*

- Proud, motivational, educational.

- Storytelling + Qur’anic guidance where natural.

- Connect present to Muslim legacy.

**Example:** “From coffee to robotics, Muslim innovators shaped the modern world. At Ummah Tech, we carry that legacy forward—harnessing technology for good.”

---

## **Language & Vocabulary**

**Use often:** ecosystem, innovation, niyyah, impact, ummah, humanity, collaboration, barakah, purpose-driven.

**Avoid:** corporate jargon (*synergy, paradigm shift*), empty hype (*biggest ever, groundbreaking* without substance), heavy inaccessible terms.

---

## **Audience Focus**

- **Tech professionals & founders:** Professional + visionary tone.
- **Muslim community & youth:** Community + inspirational tone.
- **Sponsors & partners:** Professional + visionary tone.
- **Volunteers & team:** Community tone.

---

## **Consistency Rules**

- **Greetings:** *As-salāmu ʿalaykum* (community) / *Hello* (professional).
- **Closings:** *With duʿās,* / *Warm regards,* / *The Ummah Tech Team.*
- **Qur’anic references:** Include translation + citation.
- **Arabic terms:** Italicize on first use (*niyyah (intention)*).

---

## **Do’s & Don’ts**

✅ Do: - Inspire + uplift. - Be professional but approachable. - Tie messages back to mission & service to Ummah. - Use legacy & Qur’anic inspiration meaningfully.

❌ Don’t: - Overuse hype or jargon. - Mix tones inconsistently. - Overload with Islamic references where not needed.

---

## **Voice at a Glance**

**Ummah Tech speaks with purpose, warmth, and pride.**

- **Professional** when addressing the world.

- **Collaborative** when engaging the community.

- **Inspirational** when sharing legacy & vision.
