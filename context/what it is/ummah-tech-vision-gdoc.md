**Vision**
Ummah Tech functions as a movement aimed at fostering collaboration and innovation among Muslim technology professionals. The initiative seeks to "encourage collaboration, innovation and upskilling amongst muslims in the technology sector to benefit the muslim ummah, and society at large."

**Quranic Foundation**
The organization grounds its mission in Islamic teachings, particularly Qur'an 5:2, which emphasizes cooperation in righteousness and avoiding transgression.

**Core Motivation**
- Muslim technologists remain numerous but sometimes lack organizational cohesion
- Technology embeds specific values and worldviews within its design
- Recent events (Gaza conflict) demonstrated both challenges faced by Muslim tech professionals and the power of networked communities to create solutions
- Major tech platforms have significant influence over information flow and narratives

**Guiding Principles**
The organization operates as non-sectarian, intentional in its approach, and community-impact focused.

**Planned Activities**
- In-person networking events and conferences
- Online and offline professional networks
- Educational programs and mentorship
- Research into technology and Islamic principles
- Content creation across multiple formats

**Initial Events**
A one-day technology conference scheduled for November 23, 2024, in Johannesburg, plus roundtable discussions.

**Participation Opportunities**
Interested individuals can apply as speakers, volunteers, or partners through provided contact methods.
