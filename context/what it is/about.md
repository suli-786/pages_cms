About Us
At Ummah Tech, we believe in planting the seeds today to cultivate a future where technology serves the Ummah and all of humanity.

Our Values
Our foundation is built upon Islamic principles that guide how we innovate, collaborate, and serve:

1. Niyyah (Sincere Intention) – Every action must be driven by a pure and purposeful intention to benefit the Ummah and humanity.  
2. Serving the Ummah & Humanity – Our work is dedicated to addressing real challenges and inspiring technology that addresses challenges the Muslim ummah faces and uplifts communities.  
3. Ethics & Integrity – We uphold Islamic morals and ensure that the technology we build is rooted in justice and fairness.  
4. Collaboration & Empowerment – We believe in the strength of unity, fostering relationships that empower individuals and organizations.  
5. Creativity & Innovation – We encourage futuristic thinking, ensuring that Muslim technologists remain at the forefront of global tech advancements. 
6. Connecting & Serving– We are committed to bridging gaps, building networks, and serving the needs of the Ummah.

THE CHALLENGE

• Big Tech is displacing public institutions, and exploiting user data
• Algorithms(incl. AI) are shaping behaviour, values, & even undermine faith and social cohesion

• How do we secure our independence?

CORE
IDEAS/PROPOSITIONS
1. Collaboration for the Good of the Ummah and Society

• Closing the gap between individual faith and collective, transnational solidarity.

• Striving for independence in technology to secure the Ummah’s future.

Technology is not neutral. We must design and deploy it consciously, embedding Islamic values into its foundations.
