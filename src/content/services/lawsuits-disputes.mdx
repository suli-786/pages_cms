**The work** — High-stakes commercial litigation across federal and state courts. We're a trial firm, not a paper firm — every matter is built to a record we are prepared to argue to verdict.

**What we cover** — Multi-district antitrust class actions. Securities fraud and shareholder derivative actions. Bet-the-company commercial contract disputes. Trade-secret theft and non-compete enforcement. False Claims Act qui tam defense.

**How we work** — A named partner takes the first call and stays on the file. We draft case theory before discovery opens, so every motion that follows points somewhere. The team that takes the depositions and prepares the witnesses is the team that argues in front of the jury — there are no hand-offs at trial.

**Recent matter** — A Fortune 100 wholesale energy producer in a multi-district class action alleging price-fixing across the regional spot market. Class certification denied. Action dismissed with prejudice. $4.2B exposure averted.
