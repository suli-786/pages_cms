The Ummah Tech Vision
Ummah Tech is a growing community that seeks to nurture collaboration, inspire innovation, and build skills among Muslims in the technology sector, with the aim of benefiting the Ummah and humanity.

We create spaces where Muslim technologists, founders, and creatives can come together to share ideas, learn from one another, and develop technology rooted in Islamic values. Together, we can take on the challenges facing our communities and the wider world.

Through this community, we believe ideas can grow into solutions – and solutions can drive meaningful change.

Ummah Tech Conference
Our flagship event, the annual Ummah Tech Conference usually takes place in November, rotating between Johannesburg and Cape Town. It is a gathering of founders, technologists and innovators to network and share knowledge. Our goal is to build a thriving community in South Africa that is focused on using technology to make a positive impact on society
